FAQ

Ease your curiosity with our FAQ responses

      
My child is eager to act on stage but lacks real experience and formal training. Are your programs suitable for her?
+
Absolutely. We have one top-performing class, our Showcase Cast, which utilizes an audition-only casting process each semester. Other than that, all our programs are designed to bring out the confident performer in each child, regardless of experience or current ability level. We are continually adjusting and restructuring our classes to address the needs of the students and are dedicated to focusing on the individual development of each child first and foremost.
I’m concerned that my child will just be sitting around in class doing nothing while the principal characters are rehearsing. How does your company address this issue?
+
Whenever any cast is rehearsing for a show, there are times when some cast members are not required on stage. This issue is a top priority in our curriculum development, and we continually work to minimize wasted class time. We utilize skill-building activities, have multiple instructors for simultaneous rehearsals, offer smaller classes, encourage self-initiated activities like memorizing lines, and involve students in set and costume projects during downtime. Our focus is on personal responsibility and creating a learning environment where every student can progress in their performance abilities.
Where will my child’s shows be performed at the end of the semester?
+
At our studio, the same place we hold classes every week. We have a 125 person audience capacity theater fully equipped with wireless body mics, sound system, stage lighting, and an endless inventory of costumes, props, and sets on hand right there in our 5400sq. ft. warehouse. The students practice every week on the same stage they perform on and we are able to keep production costs down by providing our own venue.
Where can I find full show details, materials, and audition instructions?
+
Full information, show materials, and instructions for submitting video auditions are available in each class's SLACK app workspaces. After registration, you will receive an email invitation to join your workspace and get started.
How does Seedling Theatre Company involve families in the theatrical journey?
+
We welcome family involvement with open arms, offering various opportunities for volunteering and participation in special performances and events. Additionally, we are committed to reserving Sundays exclusively for families, ensuring quality time for everyone.
How does Seedling Theatre Company prioritize the personal growth of each child?
+
While the thrill of performances is undeniable, we believe that every moment in class or rehearsal is an opportunity to learn. Our commitment to continuous learning pushes us to make the most of every instructional hour, providing a wealth of knowledge, experiences, and skills beyond the spotlight.
What makes the teaching approach at Seedling Theatre Company unique?
+
Our programs are meticulously designed to build confidence, enhance communication skills, and illuminate the unique talents within each child, fostering an environment where they can shine and inspire others
